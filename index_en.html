Who Are We

MINE Photography Clan (MPC) is a native Japanese photography company, which is located in Tokyo. We name our company “Clan” because our aim is to overcome and break the traditional concept of taking photos for guests --- Polishing in Studio, instead, we would like to seek people who love shooting, enjoy being memorized and cherishing the fantastic life and we regard them as our same clan. As a consequence, we devote ourselves to the memories which are made by both our guests and our company in the hasty years. Since we have thrown the dull and passive feeling of the traditional photographing type, every guest in MPC can have a real experience on communication about photography art with your photographer, stylist and cosmetician on an equal platform as well as on our hospitable service.
